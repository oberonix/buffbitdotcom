YapSauce Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

YapSauce is a voice-to-text keyboard application that processes all data entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No audio data is recorded, stored, or transmitted beyond the immediate transcription session. Audio is processed in memory and discarded after transcription.
  • No text data from your transcriptions is collected or transmitted.
  • No usage analytics or telemetry are collected.
  • No advertising identifiers are used.
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are employed.
  • No third-party services receive any data from this app.

On-Device Processing

All speech recognition and text processing is performed locally on your device using on-device AI models:

  • Speech recognition is powered by whisper.cpp, running entirely on your device's CPU.
  • Grammar correction is powered by llama.cpp, also running entirely on your device's CPU.
  • No internet connection is required for any processing features.

The only network activity occurs when you choose to download AI models from within the app. These downloads are fetched directly from Hugging Face (huggingface.co) model repositories.

Permissions

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Required to capture your voice for transcription. Audio is processed in real-time and immediately discarded after transcription. No audio is stored or transmitted.
  • Internet (INTERNET): Used only for downloading AI models. Not required for transcription.
  • Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE): Used for audio recording during voice input sessions.

Data Storage

  • AI models are stored on your device's local storage. You can delete them at any time from within the app or by clearing app data.
  • App preferences (theme selection, model choices, settings) are stored locally using Android's DataStore.
  • Transcription history is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time.

No data is stored on external servers.

Third-Party Services

YapSauce does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app does not contain ads.

AI model files are hosted on Hugging Face. Model downloads are subject to Hugging Face's privacy policy. No personal data is sent to Hugging Face — only standard HTTP requests to download model files.

Children's Privacy

YapSauce does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users. Since no data is collected from any user, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version at this URL and update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact
